Busy

The basic idea behind _busy is to put a to-do list in a convenient place that makes tasks hard to forget about.

The upper-limit on items on a to-do list is enforced to reduce the stress typically associated with to-do lists.

The idea isn't to have a huge, insurmountable to-do list that will just let you procrastinate further, but rather have an organizational tool that allows you to break down larger tasks into digestable fragments; hence the dichotomy between a 'Daily List' and a 'Weekly List'.
